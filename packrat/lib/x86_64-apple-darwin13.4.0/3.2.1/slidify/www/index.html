--- title: Slidify Playground subtitle: Adapted from OpenCPU MarkdownApp author: Ramnath Vaidyanathan framework: io2012 widgets: [mathjax] --- ## Normal Distribution The normal (or Gaussian) distribution is defined as follows: $$latex f(x;\mu,\sigma^2) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{ -\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2 } $$ --- ## Density Plot To generate random draws from a normal distribution we use the **rnorm** function: ```{r block1} output <- rnorm(1000, 100, 15); ``` --- ## Bell Shape The normal distribution has the typical bell shape: ```{r block2, fig.width=8, fig.height=5} library(ggplot2) qplot(output) ``` --- ## Kernel density estimation We can perform density estimation on the sample: ```{r block3, fig.width=8, fig.height=5} plot(density(output)) ``` --- ## Carl Friedrich GauÃŸ This little guy had something to do with it !['Gauss'](http://goo.gl/ZwTTXv)
